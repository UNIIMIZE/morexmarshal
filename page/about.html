THE MOREX
LEGACY

We started in a small workshop with a single goal: to build sports equipment that refuses to compromise on quality, performance, or soul.

OUR PILLARS

MASTER CRAFTSMANSHIP

Every bat is hand-pressed, every racket meticulously strung. We believe in the human touch that mass production simply cannot replicate.

UNYIELDING PERFORMANCE

Our gear undergoes rigorous stress testing. If it doesn't perform flawlessly on the pitch or court, it doesn't leave our factory.

FOR THE LOVE OF PLAY

Whether it's a Sunday morning cricket match or an intense carrom duel, we make equipment that honors the spirit of the game.

MADE IN INDIA.

India is a country obsessed with sports, yet premium equipment is often imported and overpriced. We changed that. For decades, athletes had to choose between affordable sub-par gear and elite equipment that broke the bank. Morex was founded to bridge this exact gap.

Sourcing the finest English Willow, aerospace-grade graphite, and solid Indian wood, we manufacture our gear locally to ensure elite quality is accessible to every passionate player. Every piece of equipment is meticulously crafted by local artisans who understand the soul of the game.

By keeping our supply chain tight and production within our borders, we eliminate exorbitant import duties and middlemen. The result? Professional-grade sports gear that champions performance, durability, and true Indian craftsmanship.
